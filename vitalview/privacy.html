Privacy Policy — VitalView

Last updated: 5 April 2026

This privacy policy explains how VitalView handles your data.

Data Controller

Jason O'Donoghue, independent developer of VitalView

Contact: privacy.vitalview@icloud.com

Summary

  • VitalView accesses health data from Apple Health (HealthKit) only with your permission.
  • Data is used solely within the app to display metrics and trends.
  • Health data is not collected, stored, or transmitted by the developer.
  • No data is sold or used for advertising or tracking.

Health Data

With your explicit permission, VitalView may read health and fitness data from Apple Health using HealthKit. This may include metrics such as heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV), VO₂ max, respiratory rate, blood oxygen levels, steps, and similar data.

This data is used exclusively to display information and trends within the app. VitalView does not write data to Apple Health.

Health data is not used for advertising, marketing, profiling, or tracking purposes.

How Your Data Is Used

  • To display health metrics and trends within the app
  • To support app functionality and user interface features

All processing takes place on your device.

Data Storage

  • On-device: Data accessed from Apple Health is processed locally on your device.
  • iCloud (if applicable): If you use iCloud, data may be stored in your Apple account according to Apple’s services.

Data Collection by the Developer

The developer does not collect, store, or transmit your personal or health data. VitalView does not use analytics, tracking tools, or third-party data collection services.

Third Parties

VitalView relies on Apple services for HealthKit and, if used, iCloud and payments. Apple may process your data according to its own privacy policy:

https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Your Rights

You can control access to your health data at any time via your device settings under Privacy & Security → Health. You may revoke permissions at any time.

Security

VitalView uses Apple’s secure frameworks and does not transmit your data externally. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and Apple ID.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date.
