Privacy Policy — Sensra

Last updated: 5 April 2026

This privacy policy explains how Sensra handles your data.

Data Controller

Jason O'Donoghue, independent developer of Sensra

Contact: Email

Summary

  • Sensra accesses health data from Apple Health (HealthKit) only with your permission.
  • Data is used solely within the app to display metrics and trends.
  • Health data is not collected, stored, or transmitted by the developer.
  • No data is sold or used for advertising or tracking.

Health Data

With your explicit permission, Sensra may read health and fitness data from Apple Health using HealthKit. This may include metrics such as heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV), VO₂ max, respiratory rate, blood oxygen levels, steps, sleep, weight, blood pressure, and similar data.

How Data Is Used

Health data is used only within the app to display current values, charts, trends, summaries, widgets, complications, and related health insights on your device.

Sensra does not use your health data for advertising, profiling, or marketing.

Storage and Transmission

Sensra does not collect, store, or transmit your HealthKit data to the developer or to external servers for processing.

Where data is shown in the app, it is read from Apple Health on your device, subject to the permissions you have granted.

Permissions

You control whether Sensra can access HealthKit data. You can change or revoke permissions at any time in the Apple Health app or in iOS settings.

Third Parties

Sensra does not sell personal data and does not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs to monetise your information.

Children’s Privacy

Sensra is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page, with the updated date shown above.

Contact
