Privacy Policy — Snaglite

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Thank you for using Snaglite. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data Controller

Snaglite (independent developer)

Contact: privacy.snaglite@icloud.com

If you use iCloud syncing, Apple may act as a separate data controller for processing performed under your Apple ID. Apple’s privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Summary

  • We collect only the data necessary to operate the app.
  • Your project data (sites, units, areas, snags, notes, attachments) is stored on your device and, if enabled, synced via Apple services under your Apple ID.
  • We do not sell your data or use your content for advertising.
  • You can export or delete your data at any time from within the app or by contacting us.

What Data We Collect

1) Data You Provide

  • Project content you create: folders (sites), projects, units, areas, snags, statuses, descriptions, notes, and metadata you add.
  • Optional attachments: photos or files you attach to items.
  • App preferences: appearance mode, onboarding completion, and other settings.

This data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud (or device backups), your data may be stored on Apple’s servers associated with your Apple ID.

2) Diagnostic/Usage Data

  • Basic, privacy‑friendly diagnostics may be logged locally to improve reliability and user experience.
  • We do not use third‑party analytics SDKs.
  • If Apple device analytics are enabled at the system level, Apple may collect aggregated usage data. See your device settings and Apple’s privacy policy.

3) Special Categories

Snaglite is not intended to collect special categories of personal data (GDPR Art. 9). If you include personal or sensitive information in notes or attachments, you are responsible for ensuring you have the right to process that data.

Legal Bases for Processing

  • Performance of a contract (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)): To provide the app’s core functionality.
  • Legitimate interests (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)): To maintain and improve reliability, quality, and security.
  • Consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)): When you opt into optional features like iCloud sync.

How We Use Your Data

  • To operate core features: create, edit, view, and manage sites, units, areas, and snags.
  • To maintain app reliability: local diagnostics and event logging (non‑identifying).
  • To improve user experience: remember your preferences.
  • To provide optional syncing: if enabled, your data is synced through Apple’s services for availability across your devices.

We do not use your data for targeted advertising and we do not sell your data.

Subscriptions and Billing

Snaglite offers auto‑renewable subscriptions and may include a 7‑day free trial. Purchases are processed by Apple. After the trial, your subscription automatically renews unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel your subscription in your device settings under Apple ID → Subscriptions. We receive purchase status information (e.g., active/expired) to unlock features. Refunds are handled by Apple according to their policies.

Data Storage and Transfers

  • Local storage: Your content and settings are stored on your device using Apple’s Core Data.
  • iCloud (optional): If enabled, your data is stored on Apple’s servers and may be transferred internationally according to Apple’s safeguards.
  • Backups: If you back up your device to iCloud or a computer, your app data may be included in those backups.

Data Retention

On‑device data persists until you delete it via the app or uninstall the app. If iCloud sync is enabled, synced data persists until you disable sync and/or delete the data from iCloud. Local diagnostic logs (if any) are retained only as long as necessary for troubleshooting and quality improvements.

Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Right of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection.
  • Right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise your rights, use in‑app tools or contact us at privacy.snaglite@icloud.com. We may request information to verify your identity.

Children’s Privacy

Snaglite is not directed to children under 16. If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child under 16, please contact us so we can delete it.

Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including Apple’s secure storage frameworks. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and Apple ID.

Third Parties

  • Apple Inc.: Provides iCloud and payment processing. See Apple’s privacy policy.
  • We do not share your personal data with other third parties except when required by law or with your explicit consent.

International Transfers

If you enable iCloud, your data may be processed and stored by Apple in data centers outside your country, subject to Apple’s safeguards.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date and may be communicated in‑app. Continued use of the app after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
